Pro Tips for Great Thumbnails

High Contrast

Use contrasting colors between text and background for maximum readability on all devices.

Large Text

Keep text large and bold - thumbnails appear small on mobile devices and in YouTube feeds.

Show Emotion

Use expressive faces, bright colors, and compelling visuals to trigger emotional responses.

Mobile First

Design for mobile viewing - most YouTube traffic comes from smartphones and tablets.

Brand Colors

Use consistent colors across your thumbnails to build brand recognition and channel identity.

Less Is More

Avoid cluttered designs - focus on one main message that viewers can understand instantly.
